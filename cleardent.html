ClearDent Case Study

Project Summary

ClearDent is a dental practice management software provider based in Vancouver, with over 40% market shares in Western Canada. I have helped ClearDent to completely overhaul the company's website as an in-house design lead, working rigorously as a team with the Revenue (Sales & Marketing) department. 

Team Members

  • Chief Revenue Officer
  • Senior Marketing Specialist
  • Outsourced Marketing Consultant
  • Design Lead

Timeline

September - December 2018 (12 weeks)

My Role

As the design lead in the project, my primary responsibilities were in visual design, web development and project management. 

Problems & Challenges

  • Prominent user-flow/usability issues
  • Incoherent and outdated design elements
